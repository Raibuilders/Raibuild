About Us

Our Company, RAI BUILDERS PTE. LIMITED, is a Construction Company incorporated on 28th January 2015. Though new, our Company has quickly spread its wings and is moving towards its goal.

In the short period of time, we have had the opportunity to prove our quality services and expertise to our Contractors. Our dedication and team effort have brought us to this level.

We are a BCA Registered Company and have attained Biz Safe Star. We are also a MOM Approved Scaffold Contractor and are certified ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 Registered Company.

Our Company currently has projects with leading Main Contractors like SHIMIZU, HYUNDAI, DRAGAGES SINGAPORE, WOH HUP (PRIVATE) LIMITED, TIONG SENG CONTRACTORS, KAJIMA, SAMSUNG, and others.

We have undertaken many challenging projects and supervise the quality of work closely. RAI BUILDERS PTE. LIMITED believes in maintaining long-term relationships with our partners and fellow workers.
